France

France, in Western Europe, Paris its capital, is known worldwide for its classical art museums including the Louvre and monuments like the Eiffel Tower.

The country is also renowned for its sophisticated cuisine and its wines. Lascaux’s ancient cave drawings, Lyon’s Roman theater and the immense Palace of Versailles are testaments to its long history.
